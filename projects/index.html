Projects

Selected Projects and Program Leadership

A snapshot of multi-country projects spanning vaccine effectiveness, transmission dynamics, and clinical development in low- and middle-income settings.

LC16m8 Mpox Vaccine Effectiveness and Safety (DRC)

Role: Scientific and Technical Lead | Funder: CEPI | Period: 2025-2028

Leading implementation and evaluation of mpox vaccine effectiveness and safety among individuals aged one year and older in the Democratic Republic of Congo.

Focus areas include protocol harmonization, field implementation, safety monitoring, and evidence translation for policy and preparedness.

Expanding Access to COVID-19 Vaccines in Africa (ECOVA)

Role: Co-Investigator and Project Technical Lead | Funder: CEPI | Period: 2020-2024

Supported multicountry studies in Madagascar and Mozambique to evaluate COVID-19 vaccine effectiveness, safety, and immunogenicity in real-world and trial settings.

The program generated practical evidence on vaccine performance and contributed to closing research gaps in underrepresented settings during the pandemic.

PREGART: HIV Treatment in Pregnancy and Breastfeeding

Role: Principal Investigator | Funder: EDCTP2 (EU Horizon 2020) | Period: 2018-2023

Led a randomized non-inferiority trial evaluating antiretroviral treatment strategies for pregnant and breastfeeding women to strengthen maternal and neonatal outcomes.

The project combined clinical research, multicenter coordination, and policy-relevant evidence generation for HIV care programs.
